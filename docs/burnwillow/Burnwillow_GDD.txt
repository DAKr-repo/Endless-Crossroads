BURNWILLOW
Game Design Document & System Reference
Version: 1.0
Genre: Tabletop Roguelike / Dungeon Crawler
Core Hook: "You are what you wear."

1. CORE PHILOSOPHY
Burnwillow is a procedural RPG where character class is defined entirely by equipment. There is no XP, no leveling up, and death is permanent. Progression comes from finding better gear (Loot) and unlocking ancient blueprints (Memory Seeds) for future runs.
The goal is to save Emberhome, a village nestled in the roots of the Burnwillow (the World-Tree), from an encroaching fungal infection known as The Rot.

2. THE SYSTEM (SRD)
The Dice Mechanic
    • The Roll: [Dice Pool] + [Stat Modifier] vs. [Difficulty Class (DC)].
    • The Pool: Characters always start with 1d6 (Base). Equipment adds additional d6s.
    • The Cap: A player may never roll more than 5d6 for a single action.

Character Stats
Generated by rolling 4d6 drop lowest.
    1. MIGHT (Physical): Strength, melee combat, athletics.
    2. WITS (Mental): Perception, ranged accuracy, technology, mechanisms.
    3. GRIT (Fortitude): Health, stamina, poison resistance.
    4. AETHER (Magical): Willpower, spellcasting, attunement.
    • Stat Mods: 8-9 (-1), 10-11 (0), 12-13 (+1), 14-15 (+2), 16-17 (+3), 18 (+4).
    • Health: 10 + Grit Mod.
    • Defense: 10 + Wits Mod (Modified by Shield/Armor).
Combat Actions
    • Ambush: Leader rolls Wits vs. Enemy Passive DC to gain a surprise round.
    • Attack: Roll [Weapon Dice] + Stat vs. Enemy Defense.
    • Active Support: Specific gear allows Actions like Intercept (Shield), Command (Banner), or Sanctify (Relic).

3. THE WORLD & LORE
The Setting
    • The Burnwillow: The universe is a single massive tree. Its leaves are living fire, providing the only light and warmth.
    • Emberhome: The player hub. A village of scavengers living in the petrified roots.
    • The Blight (The Rot): A fungal corruption rising from the deep dark. It turns living things into Hollows.
The Technology: "Arborist Tech"
Ancient civilizations didn't forge metal; they sang wood into shapes harder than steel and wove light into silk. Modern players scavenge these Relics.
    • Aether: The golden sap of the tree. Used as magic fuel.
    • Amber: Hardened Aether, used to encase powerful items or serve as power sources.
The Game Loop
    1. The Delve: Enter the procedural dungeon (The Roots).
    2. The Doom Clock: Every 10 minutes (1 Dungeon Turn) advances the Rot.
    3. Death: Permanent. The character becomes a Hollow.
    4. Legacy: Survivors bring back Memory Seeds (Blueprints) to Emberhome, allowing future characters to craft Legendary gear using Resin.

4. THE ARMORY (Gear & Loot)
Equipment Slots (10): Head, Shoulders, Chest, Arms, Legs, R. Hand, L. Hand, R. Ring, L. Ring, Neck.
Gear Tiers (Material = Dice Pool)
    • Tier I (Scrap/Wood): Adds +1d6.
    • Tier II (Iron/Leather): Adds +2d6.
    • Tier III (Steel/Silver): Adds +3d6.
    • Tier IV (Mithral/Gold): Adds +4d6.
    • Tier V (Burnwillow/Light): Adds +5d6.
Starter Gear (Tier I Examples)
    • Ironbark Cudgel: (+1d6 Might) Simple blunt weapon.
    • Amber-Tip Wand: (+1d6 Aether) Fires bolts of heat. Range: Near.
    • Burglar's Hood: (+1d6 Wits) Grants [Trap Sense].
    • Pot Lid Shield: (+1 Defense) Allows [Intercept] action.
    • Dowsing Rod: (Tool) Hums when near Secrets or Vaults.
Secrets & Vaults
    • The Veins: Shortcuts that skip a level but cost resources (HP/Keys) to open.
    • Amber Vaults: Sealed rooms containing Tier III+ loot.
    • Memory Seeds: Blueprints found in dungeons. Must be carried out to unlock crafting recipes (e.g., The Phoenix-Feather Cloak).

5. THE BESTIARY
Scaling Rule: For every Zone Depth, enemies gain +2 Defense and +1 Damage.
The Rot-Beetle (Minion)
    • DC: 8 | HP: 4 | Dmg: 2
    • Special: Skitter. -1d6 to Ranged Attacks against them.
The Hollowed Scavenger (Standard)
    • DC: 12 | HP: 10 | Dmg: 1d6 + 2
    • Special: Muscle Memory. Uses the abilities of the gear they are holding (e.g., Shields, Wands).
The Hollowed Bear (Boss - Zone 1)
    • DC: 14 | HP: 25 | Dmg: 1d6 + 4
    • Special: Terrifying Roar. Players must roll Grit vs DC 12 or be Shaken (-1d6 to rolls).
    • Special: Thrash. Attacks all adjacent targets.

6. MODULE 0: "THE ROOTS OF EMBERHOME"
Goal: Tutorial Run. Defend the village breach, equip gear, and enter Zone 1.
Scene 1: The Breach
    • Event: Floor explodes. 3 Rot-Beetles attack.
    • Lesson: Teaches basic combat math and the need for armor (DR).
Scene 2: The Armory
    • Event: Elder Kaelen opens the cache. Players pick their roles/gear.
    • Lesson: Teaches "Gear is Class."
Scene 3: The Fork
    • Path A (The Teased Vault): A Tier III Sun-Bow visible behind unbreakable Amber. Teaches "Metroidvania" progression (Come back later with better tools).
    • Path B (The Secret): A Dowsing Rod or Search Check reveals a hidden cache (Resin/Scrolls). Teaches Exploration.
Scene 4: The Boss
    • Event: Battle with the Hollowed Bear at the root tear.
    • Lesson: Teaches Boss mechanics and active defense.
