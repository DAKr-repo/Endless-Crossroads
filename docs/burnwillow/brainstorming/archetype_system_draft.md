# Archetype System — Brainstorm Draft (Removed 2026-03-28)

This was a proposed system where equipping enough gear in a stat pool would trigger "archetype" abilities. It was removed because it contradicts the core design principle: **all abilities come from gear**. The archetype system created invisible skill trees that existed outside of equipment, which breaks "You Are What You Wear."

The concept of emergent playstyles is preserved through Example Builds in the sourcebook and setting bible. The mechanical ideas below may be repurposed as **gear traits on specific items** in the future.

---

## Original Design

Archetypes were emergent identities that arose from how a player equipped their GearGrid. Equip enough gear feeding a particular stat pool and the archetype would activate, granting passive and active abilities. Archetypes could shift mid-run as gear changes.

### Vanguard (Might-Heavy Frontline)

Triggers when 3+ equipped items feed the Might pool.

| Unlock | Ability | Effect |
|--------|---------|--------|
| 2 Might items | Cleaving Momentum | Kill an enemy, next attack gets +1d6 |
| 3 Might items | Staggering Blow | Attacks have 20% chance to stun for 1 round |
| 4 Might items | Warcry | Once per combat: all enemies -2 defense for 2 rounds |
| 5 Might items | Unstoppable | First lethal hit per floor reduces you to 1 HP instead |
| Exalted: Dam-Wrights | Fortification | Barricades you build have double HP |

### Sentinel (Grit-Heavy Tank/Protector)

Triggers when 3+ equipped items feed the Grit pool.

| Unlock | Ability | Effect |
|--------|---------|--------|
| 2 Grit items | Brace | +1 DR when you did not move this turn |
| 3 Grit items | Bodyguard | Adjacent ally takes 50% less damage from AoE |
| 4 Grit items | Retribution | When hit, attacker takes 1d4 damage |
| 5 Grit items | Immovable | Cannot be moved, knocked prone, or stunned |
| Exalted: Heartwood Elders | Root-Graft | Grafted armor regenerates 1 DR per floor |

### Arcanist (Aether-Heavy Caster)

Triggers when 3+ equipped items feed the Aether pool.

| Unlock | Ability | Effect |
|--------|---------|--------|
| 2 Aether items | Resonance | Trait abilities get -1 DC |
| 3 Aether items | Afterburn | Elemental damage lingers: +1d4 next round |
| 4 Aether items | Chain Cast | On trait success, 30% chance to cast again free |
| 5 Aether items | Aether Overflow | Excess dice beyond 5d6 cap become flat +2 per die |
| Exalted: Canopy Court | Silk Resonance | Silk-enchanted items cost half materials |

### Shadow (Wits-Heavy Precision/Utility)

Triggers when 3+ equipped items feed the Wits pool.

| Unlock | Ability | Effect |
|--------|---------|--------|
| 2 Wits items | Exploit Weakness | +1d6 damage vs enemies with any status condition |
| 3 Wits items | Trapmaster | SET_TRAP creates 2 hazards instead of 1 |
| 4 Wits items | Ambush Predator | First attack in any combat is automatically a crit |
| 5 Wits items | Ghost Step | Once per floor: skip a room entirely (bypass all encounters) |
| Exalted: The Hive | Hive-Sight | Royal Jelly also reveals enemy weaknesses |

### Herbalist (Healing Trait Focus)

Triggers when 2+ equipped items have healing-related traits.

| Unlock | Ability | Effect |
|--------|---------|--------|
| 2 healing traits | Efficient Triage | Heal abilities get +1d6 |
| 3 healing traits | Preventive Care | Party starts each combat with 1d4 temp HP |
| 4 healing traits | Overheal | Excess healing becomes temp HP (caps at 5) |
| Exalted: Mycelium | Panacea | Potions cure ALL conditions, not just one |
| Exalted: Hag Circle | Rot Sympathy | Blight damage against you heals instead (once per floor) |

### Beastmaster (Summon/Minion Focus)

Triggers when [Summon] trait is equipped and 1+ minion is active.

| Unlock | Ability | Effect |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Summon + 1 minion | Pack Bond | You and minion both get +1d6 attacking same target |
| 2+ minion items | Feral Instinct | Ambush checks get +3 |
| Exalted: any beast faction | Alpha Call | Summon 2 minions instead of 1 |
| Full Beastmaster | Symbiosis | When minion dies, gain its remaining HP as temp HP |

---

## Why It Was Removed

The abilities above (Cleaving Momentum, Staggering Blow, Warcry, etc.) don't come from any piece of gear. They come from a meta-system that counts items. This creates invisible power that exists outside the GearGrid, which violates the core design.

## How to Repurpose

These abilities could become:
1. **New gear traits** — e.g., a weapon with [Warcry] or armor with [Unstoppable]
2. **Set bonuses** — the Warden's Watch 4-piece bonus could grant a retribution effect
3. **Affix effects** — a "...of Retribution" suffix that reflects damage
4. **Faction reward gear** — the Dam-Wright Exalted reward could be a specific piece of equipment with the Fortification trait, not a passive ability
